The Problem

Young adults do not effectively plan meals; they make impulse purchases, and thus contribute to 47% of avoidable food waste.

The Solution

To encourage zero food waste practices and to reduce overall food waste by changing consumer purchasing habits.

Features

Pocket Pantry is an all-in-one meal planner and grocery list web app that was designed to help you reduce waste!

Plan your meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner with our easy-to-use meal planner!

Generate grocery lists based on your meal plans, so that you know exactly what you need!
